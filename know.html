    Poultry Feeding Schedules

    Broilers

    Starter Feeds

    Starter feeds are specifically formulated to meet the nutritional requirements of broiler chicks during their initial growth phase, typically from day one until around 4-6 weeks of age. These feeds play a critical role in laying the foundation for healthy growth and development during the early stages of life.

    Grower Feeds

    Introduced after the starter stage, typically around 4-6 weeks of age, and continued until the birds reach market weight. These feeds support steady growth and muscle development in broilers during the intermediate stage of their growth cycle.

    Layers

    Starter Feeds

    Administered to young pullets from hatch until around 6-8 weeks of age. These feeds are crucial for laying the foundation for future egg production by providing essential nutrients during the early stages of growth.

    Grower Feeds

    Given to pullets from around 6-8 weeks of age until they begin laying eggs, usually around 16-20 weeks of age. These feeds help support proper skeletal development and prepare the birds for the onset of egg production.

    Finisher Feeds

    Provided to laying hens once they begin egg production, usually around 16-20 weeks of age and throughout their laying cycle. These feeds are formulated to meet the increased energy and nutrient requirements of laying hens to support consistent egg production and quality.
